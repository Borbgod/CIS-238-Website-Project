The Best Resturants in Michigan!

Detroit: Wright & Company

Wright & Company is a gastro-bistro located on the second floor of the historic Wright-Kay building in downtown Detroit. We serve contemporary American small plates, craft cocktails, natural and biodynamic wines and craft beers from around the world. Our entrance is on the John R side of the building, toward the alley.

Check out the menu!

Grand Rapids: Beer City Bread Co.

Our hand crafted food & drink inspired concept offers an exceptional atmosphere to go along with a quality of service that can’t be matched. Our menu meets the highest standards of quality with a focus on freshness with a modern twist. Our bar stands out among the rest because of its shear beauty from its 80+ feet of rustic hickory wood crafted from amish hands to its 2 tap towers hosting 24 taps a piece.

Check out the menu!

Ann Arbor: Blue Llama Jazz Club

As an established destination music venue and restaurant in Ann Arbor, Michigan, the Blue LLama Jazz Club presents live, local, and national jazz and world music. This remarkable dining and listening experience unites the “Love of Food” with the “Love of Music” with a menu of Afro-Caribbean cuisine designed by James Beard award winner Chef JJ Johnson, curated wine list, and craft cocktails.

Check out the menu!

Lansing: Meat BBQ

We are proud to serve the Lansing area with the best BBQ around! Whether you are looking for some delicious smoked chicken wings, juicy brisket, tender pulled pork or more, you are sure to find the perfect meal at Meat! Since we opened we have been proudly serving nothing but top notch food to everyone who visits, and we take pride in making sure you have a wonderful experience every time you visit the restaurant. Pair one of our delicious smoked meat plates with your favorite craft beer from our ever changing draft list. We also have a full bar to mix up your favorite cocktails, as well as homemade drinks like our delicious sangria! Nothing washes down a big plate of BBQ better than a delicious drink, so come check out what we have on tap today! We are dedicated to providing the highest quality food and service to everyone who visits, and you will learn that the second you walk in. Looking to get some delicious Meat for lunch or dinner today? Come see us at Meat BBQ!

Check out the menu!

Kalamazoo: Bell's Eccentric Cafe

Located in beautiful downtown Kalamazoo, Michigan, the Eccentric Cafe, located next to the Bell's General Store, is home to a unique offering of craft beer, live music, entertainment, delicious food and more.Any given time, we have between 20 and 40 different beers on tap. Many are exclusive to the Cafe and are brewed right next door at our original brewery - just look for the yellow tags on the Draft Board when you visit.

Check out the menu!

Flint: Blackstone's Smokehouse

Enjoy the exposed history of the building while submersed in great food, drink and sounds! From the minute you walk in you will feel the warmth and organic nature of the dining room. The staff is full of energy and the food is craft at it's best. Not overdone, just a comfortable setting with care put into every part of your experience. Find the evening that the place has live music and you have taken your experience to a whole new level! This is the place to be in Genesee County.

Check out the menu!

Dearborn: Ford's Garage Dearborn

Ford's Garage Dearborn is your neighborhood burger and beer joint, where everyone is welcome! We're serving up 1/2 pound Black Angus Burgers, made fresh to order and never frozen, 7-days-a-week. Additionally, we offer comfort food favorites, lighter fare, and a good time!

Check out the menu!

Traverse City: Boathouse Restaurant

French Fusion cuisine nesteled on beautiful Bowers Harbor, Deck Seating available in season, Romantic location for a proposal, also available for small/medium sized weddings, come to relax and enjoy the Taste of the Big City in Traverse City. Featuring Kobe Beef, English Dover Sole and the freshest most eclectic cuisine. Come as you are, upscale dining in a resort style casual atmosphere.

Check out the menu!

Warren: Malone's Tavern

Since 2002, Malone's Tavern has been fulfilling it's mission of providing great food and excellent service in a fun yet relaxing atmosphere. The word is out about our fabulous homemade meals, ice cold beer, daily specials and friendly service. Malone's Tavern is designed to be a place where people of all walks of life gather, in good spirits, to relax and share the news of the day. You can catch your favorite game, enjoy a delicious meal and have a ice cold beverage. After years of success, Malone's expanded with an open-air, nonsmoking room with an attached outside patio. This addition makes us the perfect gathering place for your family or friends to celebrate events from office parties to wedding rehearsal dinners and everything in between. Stop in at Malone's and become part of the Buzz!!

Check out the menu!

Livonia: J Alexander's Restaurant

Come and experience an unforgettable, immersive culinary experience. Whether you visit us for lunch, dinner, or a drink with friends, we want your time at J. Alexander’s to be memorable. From the moment you walk in our doors, our friendly staff will be attentive to your every need. At the same time, our chefs are hard at work every day to deliver consistent, quality food that is sure to delight even the most refined palate.
