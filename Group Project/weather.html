Weather

  • No matter where you're traveling to, its best to prepare for the weather!
  • Look below for frequently visited cities, and for more information about a specific city, visit weather.com

Michigan's seasons can be hard to plan around, but with this season-specific information, you are in good hands!

Spring

  • Temperature: 30s – 70s °F
  • Weather: Spring starts cold, but warms up near May. A healthy dose of rain makes for a beautiful blossom!
  • Fun Fact: The Holland Tulip Time Festival takes place during this season! Check it out on our Events page

Summer

  • Temperature: 70s – 90s °F
  • Weather: The summers consist of a good mix of hot sunny days and cool days with a breeze. The beaches and piers in West Michigan are a must-visit!
  • Fun Fact: Being near the Great Lakes keeps temperatures slightly cooler along the coast and fuels some storms due to the Lake Effect.

Fall

  • Temperature: 40s – 70s °F
  • Weather: Fall is crisp and cool with beautiful fall colors but can start to get cold earlier in the season, so if you're traveling during this time, pack accordingly!
  • Fun Fact: On rare occassions, the first snow fall of the year occurs as early as October!

Winter

  • Temperature: -10 – 30s °F
  • Weather: No matter where you go in Michigan during the winter, one thing remains true: it's going to be cold. Best to bundle up, but don't forget to enjoy the beauty of a Michigan snowfall!
  • Fun Fact: In the Upper Peninsula, the aurora borealis can be seen during the winter!
