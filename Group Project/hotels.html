Top 12 Highest Rated Hotels in Michigan!

1: Hotel Walloon

Hotel Walloon, where guests can enjoy the charm and hospitality of the early twentieth century along with the comforts of every modern amenity. Settled against the breathtaking backdrop of Walloon Lake, this independently owned and operated boutique hotel features 32 unique, elegant and comfortable guest rooms. Centrally located between Petoskey, Boyne City, Charlevoix, Traverse City and Mackinaw, Hotel Walloon is the perfect "home away from home" to enjoy all the adventure Northern Michigan has to offer. They look forward to exceeding guest expectations and adhering to the highest standards of quality, hospitality and character in Northern Michigan.

Click here to book your stay at the Hotel Walloon today!

2: Superior Stay Hotel

Superior Stay Hotel offers the ultimate in contemporary comfort. Guest rooms are furnished with super clean hardwood floors, comfortable beds, high-quality linen, and a range of modern amenities such as nano-cell television, warm lighting, plenty of device charging ports, ultra-high-speed high bandwidth wifi. Explore the vibrant downtown and lakeshore of Marquette from the nearby comfort of a boutique hotel. Their boutique hotel offers a convenient location near outdoor activities, Northern Michigan University, Superior Dome on a quiet residential street, yet walkable to all the diners and bars that downtown Marquette has to offer. Their continental breakfast sets you up for a successful day of sightseeing or business.

Click here to book your stay at the Superior Stay Hotel today!

3: Emerald Island Inn

See why so many travelers make Emerald Island Inn their hotel of choice when visiting Beaver Island. Providing an ideal mix of value, comfort and convenience, it offers an array of amenities designed for travelers like you. The rooms offer a kitchenette and a refrigerator, and getting online is possible, as free wifi is available, allowing you to rest and refresh with ease. Guests arriving by vehicle have access to free parking. When you’re feeling hungry, be sure to check out Shamrock Bar and Restaurant and Daddy Frank's, which are some pubs that are popular with locals and out-of-towners alike. Should time allow, Beaver Head Lighthouse is a popular attraction that is within walking distance. We’re sure you’ll enjoy your stay at Emerald Isle Hotel as you experience all of the things Beaver Island has to offer.

Click here to book your stay at the Emerald Island Inn today!

Guests at the Inn at Stonecliffe can sit on the mansion's front porch, look out over the Straits of Mackinac, and reflect upon the estate's interesting and checkered history.

6: Hotel David Whitney, Autograph Collection

Take your upcoming travel experience to the next level when you stay at Hotel David Whitney, Autograph Collection. Their boutique hotel is located within the historic David Whitney building in the heart of downtown Detroit next to the Grand Circus Park and near sites such as Comerica Park, Ford Field and The Fillmore. Enjoy hassle-free travel with access to the Detroit People Mover and Q-Line, accessing all of downtown Detroit's must-see attractions. Retreat to spacious rooms and suites boasting luxurious bedding and bath amenities. During downtime, head to their 24-hour fitness center to work out and meet friends or colleagues at the Library Bar to mingle over cocktails.

8: The Harrington Inn

As a 3-Diamond AAA rated hotel, their friendly staff promises that whether you select a roomy Single Queen or a Deluxe King Jacuzzi Fireplace State Room, you will have a quiet, clean, comfortable and enjoyable stay. The Harrington features a 24/7 front desk, free high-speed WiFi, business center, on-site laundry, and fitness center. At no cost, guests may use the nearby Spectrum/Tamarac Fitness Center featuring a fully equipped with an indoor pool, track and sauna. A seasonally-heated pool is on site. Enjoy a continental breakfast, afternoon fresh-baked cookies and evening popcorn. Douwe Egberts coffee, tea, juice and fresh fruit are always available. Only minutes from the Muskegon and White Rivers, four area golf courses, along with parks and walking paths. Free transportation is available to/from Fremont Airport. Lake Michigan, Michigan Adventure, wineries and Meijer Garden are an easy drive.

9: Hotel Royal Oak

Enjoy gorgeous modern rooms with a resort vibe just steps to 100's of bars, restaurants & shows in Downtown Royal Oak. Their Lobby, Sky Lounge, Pavilion, and Lawn, courtesy bicycles, fire features, hammocks, outdoor games, and much more are included in your stay. Whether you're in Holland for business, visiting your Hope College student or enjoying a family vacation, the Teerman Lofts Hotel will provide you with the best location and top amenities to help make your stay enjoyable and productive.
